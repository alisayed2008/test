Frequently Asked Questions
Why do I need to pay a deposit before ordering?
The deposit secures production start and materials. It may be refundable according to store policy.
How is my design converted into the final product?
We prepare the design for printing/embroidery and create a sample for approval before full production.
How is the product price calculated?
Price depends on size, fabric, printing/embroidery method and production quantity.
My order delivery is delayed. What should I do?
We apologize for the delay — contact us by phone or email to check the order status.
My product arrived unsatisfactory
Send a complaint with photos and we will review and propose immediate solutions.
